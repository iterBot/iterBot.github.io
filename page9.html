  • Obstacle: Column

This picture represents one of the proposed terrain layouts. The gray block represents the starting position of the creatures. The fitness score of the individuals is determined by their distance travelled along the yellow line (in the positive direction) however if they stray from the line, their fitness score is decreased. With the proposed obstacle, a creature would have to develop a mutation that would allow it to travel around the obstacle to continue increasing its score. It is expected that the majority of the creatures will peak by landing directly against the center of the column. If a creature is able to develop the ability to circumvent the column, it will be chosen and further developed as champion DNA within the algorithm.

  • Obstacle: Walls Increasing In Height

This obstacle presents a different challenge. While the other obstacles test how quickly the obstacle can be overcome, increasingly difficult terrain obstacles would test the actual limits of the simulation. For this example, the fitness would not be based on how closely the creature stays to the forward direction, but rather by how many barriers the creature manages to overcome. This different definition of fitness would test the individual on overall climbing ability rather than an ability to achieve direct forward progress.
